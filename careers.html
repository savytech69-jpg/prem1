Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience to enroll?

No prior experience is required! Our programs are designed for complete beginners. We start with fundamentals and progressively build your skills to professional level.

Is the certification recognized?

Yes, all our programs are government-approved and nationally recognized. The certification is valid across India and enhances your employability significantly.

What is the minimum age requirement?

The minimum age is 16 years. There's no upper age limit—we welcome learners of all ages who are passionate about beauty and wellness.

Are payment plans available?

Yes! We offer flexible EMI options and installment plans. You can pay in 2-4 installments depending on the program duration. Contact us for details.

Will I get job placement assistance?

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive career support including resume building, interview prep, portfolio development, and direct connections to hiring salons and spas.

What is included in the program fee?

The fee covers all training materials, product kits, certification exam, uniforms, and lifetime access to course updates. No hidden costs!
